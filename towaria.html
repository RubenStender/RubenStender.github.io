Towaria

トワリア

Overview
What is Towaria?

A cute, fast-paced tower defense with a medieval voxel art style. Designed for people who want to kill time — accessible, level-based, and forgiving. Fail a wave? Restart it with your towers already placed.

Mechanics
How it plays

Survive waves of enemies using four tower types. Earn coins from defeated enemies and spend them on new towers between waves. Each wave resets on fail — no full restart.

Towers
The four towers
Archer
Fast fire rate, low damage. Reliable against weak swarms.
Cannon
Slow but heavy. Area damage, great against tanky enemies.
Magic
Fast bursts, more damage than the archer — but pricier.
Shockwave
Minimal damage, but stuns all enemies in range for a few seconds.
Contribution
My work

UI design, enemy 3D models, the money system, health and progress bar, and the speed-up toggle. Built over ~3 weeks.
