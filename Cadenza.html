Cadenza

カデンツァ

A game of opportunity, death, and music! Based on a real story of loss. Play as Lili, a mother, wife, and someone trying to understand her surroundings. Help her come to terms with it all and decide what must come next…

What is Cadenza?

Cadenza is a walking sim/rythm game that sets a dark tone. The game is about Lili. She is an composer and a loving mother that slowly figures out the harsh truth and has to come to terms with one of the choices of all.

Some mechanics

You use WASD to move around. ZXC to play the rythm minigames. and you click to continue the dialogue. You walk around the house trying to remember the most important things in your life.



Meet the family!!!

Lili

Lili is a loving mom and music composer. She's always positive and always brightens the mood. She would always make notes for new songs in her notebook.

Joseph

Joseph is Lili's husband. They've been happily married for a while. Lili always loved his suit and tie. Her happiest day.

Sam

Sam is Lili and Joseph their daughter. She has a cute yellow duck plushie. It's her favorite.

Arin

Arin is the son of Lili and Joseph. He's a moody teen that likes music. He listens a lot through his headphones.
