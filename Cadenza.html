Overview
What is Cadenza?

A walking sim and rhythm game with a dark tone. Based on a real story of loss. Play as Lili — a mother, composer, and wife — as she pieces together the truth and faces an impossible choice.

We made Cadenza for the Jamsepticeye Halloween gamejam. Hosted by Jacksepticeye and DuckyDev. And we made the game in about 4 days.

Gamejam Link ▸
Controls
How it plays

WASD to move. ZXC for rhythm minigames. Click to continue dialogue. Walk through the house and find what matters most.
