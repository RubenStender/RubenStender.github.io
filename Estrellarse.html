Overview
What is Estrellarse?

Estrellarse is a roguelite movement shooter based on one of my own made characters with the same name. The game is based in 2045 at this time a notorious scientist kidnapped her boyfriend. And Maria goes through the city to look for her boyfriend.

▸ Download / View on GitHub
Controls
NOTE: The game is only playable with mouse and keyboard
WASD & Mouse
Use WASD to move around the world and move your mouse to look around.
Shift & Ctrl
Use Left Shift to sprint. In the game you run automatically but by using Left Ctrl you can slowwalk for trickier parkour.
Alt
Use Left Alt to use Maria's ability. She can dash with high speeds for a short period of time.
C
Use C to slide underneath low hanging objects or to add some flair to your shooting.
Space
Use space to jump. You can also hang off hanging poles. When timing the jump right you can jump across these bars like monkeybars
Characters
Meet some of the appearing characters
Maria "Estrellarse" Garcia
23 year old woman with the power to dash with great speeds. This game follows her emotional and physical journey of finding her kidnapped boyfriend.
Ricardo "Lanzado" Hernando
Maria's boyfriend. Ricardo has the ability to run at high speeds.
Papaver "Poppy-god" Somniferum
A scientist at a big bio-company who became a victim of his own creations.
