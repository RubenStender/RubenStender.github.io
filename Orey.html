Overview
What is Orey?

Orey is a arcade style, endless runner game. In Orey you play as a sentient stone called Orey. Orey exists from various precious minerals. This makes him a target for miners. Can you escape them?

Mechanics
How it plays

Run as far as possible to up your highscore. The game gets harder the longer you play. You can find power-ups on the ground that make Orey invincible.

Characters
The 3 characters
Orey
A sentient stone running from the miners that try to get his precious minerals.
Michael Miner
Chases down Orey with a pickaxe for his minerals.
Drill Bill
Surprises Orey sometimes with a big drill machine. He can shoot the drills out the machine towards Orey
Contribution
My work

UI design, enemy 3D models, the money system, health and progress bar, and the speed-up toggle. Built over ~3 weeks.
