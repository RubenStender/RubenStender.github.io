Welcome to my portfolio

I'm an 18-year-old beginner software developer from Enschede, a city in the Netherlands. I like creating stories, games, and building universes of characters. My creativity constantly gives me ideas for new characters, abilities, and places for my worldbuilding project. And it's giving me new ideas for games.

The beginning

My interest in code and worldbuilding comes from a young age. When I played my first couple of games on my first console, the Xbox 360, I loved the way games felt. How the story behind it was, how the characters looked, and how the emotions changed by all of them. When I started getting into superhero movies, I knew I wanted to create characters as well. The first couple of characters I created were harsh—not fully thought out and just overall not the best. I missed the way the game characters felt alive, and mine felt... bland. Since then, I wanted to go into either animation or game development.
