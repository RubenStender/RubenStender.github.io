The beginning

My interest in code and worldbuilding comes from a young age for me. When i played my first couple of games on my first console, the Xbox-360. I loved the way games felt. How the story behind it was, how the characters looked and how the emotions changed by all of them. When i started get into super hero movies i knew i wanted to create characters aswell. The first couple of characters i created were harsh. Not fully thought out and just overall not the best. And i missed the way the game characters felt alive and mine felt... bland. Since then i wanted to go into either animation or game development.
